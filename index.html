ABOUT

Hi, I'm Arundhati.

Lausanne, Switzerland

I'm an engineer by training, a designer by passion, and a researcher driven by curiosity.

Currently, I’m in the final stretch of my master’s at EPFL’s College of Digital Humanities, where I get to merge tech, design, art, and cognition in my work. I also work as a student assistant at Metalab at Harvard University and at The Laboratory of Cognitive Neuroscience at the Campus Biotech in Geneva.

I'm also building polyswap, an app that can be used to barter clothes between university students.

Formerly, I've worked at the Digital and Cognitive Musicology Lab (EPFL), Lopez Design, EPFL Future Leaders and the New York Academy of Sciences.

In my free time, I like to play the piano, go bouldering, draw the things around me or lose myself in a good book.
